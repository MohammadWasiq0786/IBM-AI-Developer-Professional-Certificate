Objective for Exercise:

  • Learn how different datasets affect the predictions of an AI model.
  • Learn why reducing bias in AI models is so important

Detect the Bias

Biased data may lead to biased predictions, and AI models are only as fair as their training data. It’s important to be aware of the possibility of bias, so that you can mitigate against it.

Rate the fairness of the outcomes, and see if other people agree.
